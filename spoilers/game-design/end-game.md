# End game

## Pre-requisites

Player is:
- Enlightened in all 8 virtues
- Is "At Peace" in all virtues

Player has:
- has a pale oak boat (available from Wandering Trader or by building one).
- has and is wearing full netherite armor (or some way of protecting themself while in boat with an agro'd Bellatrix)
- has a tag with name "BELAATRIX" on it (requires an anvil to have been used.)

Player knows:
- Knows Bellatrix's true name ("BELAATRIX") to summon her (tell out loud) - must done at DUSK/NIGHT when stars are out.
- Get her into the pale oak boat.  Must be pale oak.  
- Tag her with the named tag ("BELAATRIX")
- From lore: to calm Bellatrix, you must first unscramble her true name.
- Say out loud to all players the three best words to live by ("Relax a bit") while in a boat with an aggro'd BELAATRIX (player will be taking damage).

After saying "Relax a bit" out loud to all players:
- Final Goal Advancement achieve reported to all players: "Yer Hysterical!" - Calm an angry enderman
- Enderman tagged as BELAATRIX disappears.
- Automated Fireworks, ideally like with hero of village. (provide trigger called ending_fireworks for testing)
