# End Game

## Obtaining Bellatrix

The Wandering Trader sells an enderman spawn egg in exchange for a netherite sword. This is the only way to obtain one in the pack. Picking up the egg opens the **End Game** advancement tab and grants the "Bellatrix's Travel Egg!" advancement. Using the egg spawns Bellatrix into the world.

---

## Pre-requisites

Player is:
- Enlightened in all 8 virtues
- At Peace across all virtues simultaneously

Player has:
- A pale oak boat (to capture Bellatrix)
- Full netherite armor (protection while in boat once Bellatrix is aggro'd)
- A name tag inscribed with "BELAATRIX" (requires anvil)

Player knows (discovered through lore, not explained directly):
- She is known as "Bellatrix" and the stone circle is an "Abelatrix" — but her TRUE name, discovered through lore, is "BELAATRIX". Knowing her true name gives the player the letters needed to unscramble the final words: RELAX A BIT
- She must be lured into a pale oak boat — must be pale oak specifically
- She must be tagged with the "BELAATRIX" name tag
- To calm her, say the three best words to live by: *"RELAX A BIT"* — `/trigger RELAXABIT` while in the boat with her, taking damage

---

## Sequence

1. Night falls, stars are visible
2. Player uses enderman spawn egg (purchased from Wandering Trader for a netherite sword) — Bellatrix spawns
3. Player lures her into a pale oak boat
4. Player tags her with the "BELAATRIX" name tag via anvil
5. Player aggros her (look her in the eyes)
6. Player types `/trigger RELAXABIT` while in the boat and taking damage from her
7. Final sequence fires:
   - **"Yer Hysterical!"** goal advancement granted and announced to all players
   - Bellatrix (BELAATRIX-tagged enderman) despawns (vanishes as if returning to space)
   - Fireworks burst in 5 directions around the player
   - Gold broadcast to all: *"[username] has calmed the storm."*

## Notes

- The pale oak boat is the vessel used to capture Bellatrix — pale oak is rare and must be sought out or obtained from the Wandering Trader
- Netherite armor is required because the player will be taking enderman melee damage during the RELAXABIT step
- The fireworks mirror the Hero of the Village effect — celebratory, automatic, world-visible
- `virtues:dev/ending_fireworks` is the dev trigger for testing the fireworks sequence in isolation
