# The Antosh Family

A single villager type (Toolsmith) with biome-specific variants sharing a family name. Each member has unique trades that depend on items or knowledge obtained from another family member — forcing geographical travel as a virtue mechanic.

## Family members (3)

| Biome | Name | Role |
|-------|------|------|
| Plains | Brother Antosh | Holds smithing template and can give Rune of Guidance. |
| Savanna | Sister Antosh | Holds netherite ingot and can give Rune of Guidance. |
| Taiga | Elder Antosh    | Holds diamond pickaxe and can give Rune of Guidance. |

### The Mechanic

Each family member of Antosh given iron ingot (proof of knowledge of metallurgy, taught by an Armorer) and soul lantern will reveal a piece needed to yield the Rune of Guidance.  The chain forces the journey around the world — no shortcutting by trading with just one family member.

### The Virtue

The soul lantern guides the way and ultimately is a gating mechanic that yields the rune of Guidance. The player is connecting a family separated by geography. The virtue is to demonstrate the act of human connection.
