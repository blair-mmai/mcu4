# Librarian Virtue Trades

## Assignment Mechanic
When any villager claims a lectern and becomes a librarian, a datapack
function fires and randomly assigns one of 8 virtue identities to that
librarian. Identity stored via scoreboard tag or NBT marker.

## Virtue Reveal
The novice trade reveals the librarian's virtue identity — no label, no
sign. Player interprets the symbolic pairing. The novice give-item is also
the virtue's consistent identifier used across advancement tab icons,
rune crafting, and shrine offerings.

## Gating Note
L1 and L2 trades are gating trades — they require effort to complete,
not just items in hand. Mushroom soup for example requires farming,
crafting, and giving while hungry. The gate IS the virtue expression,
not just a level-up mechanism.

## Player Tagging (Future)
In multiplayer, librarians should be tagged with the first player to
interact with them — UUID or name stored in villager NBT. Subsequent
trades only complete for that player. Prevents gaming by other players.
Flagged as future mechanic — enforcement requires tick-based monitoring
of trade interactions.
