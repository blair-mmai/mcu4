# Future Ideas: The Stupid Ending

## The 9th Enlightenment

After all 8 virtue enlightenments achieved, a trapdoor opens at the
center of the Abelatrix. Player drops in.

---

## What's Down There

All of the following. In one room.

- A single chest containing one piece of cake and a sign: *"Well done."*
- A villager sitting in a chair who says *"You made it. I've been here
  the whole time."*
- The words RELAX A BIT written in wool blocks on the floor
- A bed named *"You've earned this."*
- Lord British sitting in a corner. Says *"I wondered when you'd find me."*
- A jukebox playing a disc. No other context.
- A cow. Named Freddie Mac Moo.

---

## The Tone

The stupid ending is the right ending for a virtue game. You spent the
whole time being earnest and sincere. The world rewards you with something
that doesn't take itself seriously. That IS the reward.

ABELATRIX = RELAX A BIT

The circle knew all along.
